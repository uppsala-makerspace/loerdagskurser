# About the laser cutter course


The laser cutting course is one of [the courses](https://uppsala-makerspace.github.io/loerdagskurser/kurserna)
of [the Saturday courses](https://uppsala-makerspace.github.io/loerdagskurser/).

In the laser cutting course one learns how to use a laser cutter.

The course is a self-study course for adults:
at the moment we do not have a volunteer who can teach the laser cutter
for teenagers.
