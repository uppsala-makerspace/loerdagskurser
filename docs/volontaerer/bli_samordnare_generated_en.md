
# How to become a coordinator at the Saturday courses?


[The Saturday courses](https://uppsala-makerspace.github.io/loerdagskurser/)
is lead by [volunteers](https://uppsala-makerspace.github.io/loerdagskurser/volontaerer) in different roles.
Course coordinator is one of these roles.

The course coordinator has the task to ensure all courses can run smoothly
and is end responsible.

## How can I become a course coordinator?


By letting us know :-)

Ideally, you [contact the course coordinator](https://uppsala-makerspace.github.io/loerdagskurser/kontakta_oss)
and there will be proper time for all your questions and ideas.
