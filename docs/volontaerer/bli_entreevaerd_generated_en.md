
# How to become a reception desk host at the Saturday courses?

[![Lördagskurserna logo](loerdagskurser_logo_64_x_60.png)](https://uppsala-makerspace.github.io/loerdagskurser/volontaerer/bli_entreevaerd/)

## How can I become a host?


By letting us know :-)

Ideally, you [contact the course coordinator](https://uppsala-makerspace.github.io/loerdagskurser/kontakta_oss)
and there will be proper time for all your questions and ideas.

Else show up a Lördagskurser day and,
when our guests have been taken care of,
talk to the host there.
